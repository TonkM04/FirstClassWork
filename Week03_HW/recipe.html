Food Description:

This is a homemade Bengali style chicken curry that can be easily cooked at home. It uses simple ingredients but has a rich and authentic flavor, similar to traditional Bengali home cooking.


  • Step 1: Finely chop the onion and tomato.
  • Step 2: Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Step 3: Add the onion and cook until deeply browned.
  • Step 4: Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until fragrant.
  • Step 5: Add the chopped tomato and cook until the moisture releases.
  • Step 6: Add the curry powder mix and stir until aromatic.
  • Step 7: Add the chicken drumsticks and stir-fry for about 4 minutes.
  • Step 8: Add hot water to cover the chicken and season with salt.
  • Step 9: Cover the pan and simmer for about 20 minutes.
  • Step 10: Turn off the heat and sprinkle garam masala before serving.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken drumsticks
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Ginger & garlic paste
  • Curry powder
  • Garam masala
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Water
