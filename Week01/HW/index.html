Bengali Chicken Curry

Description

This is a homemade Bengali style chicken curry that can be easily cooked at home. It uses simple ingredients but has a rich and authentic flavor, similar to traditional Bengali home cooking.

Ingredients

  • Chicken drumsticks
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Ginger & garlic mixed paste
  • Curry powder mix
  • Garam masala
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Finely chop the onion and tomato.
  2. Step 2: Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Step 3: Add the onion and cook until deeply browned.
  4. Step 4: Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until fragrant.
  5. Step 5: Add the chopped tomato and cook until the moisture releases.
  6. Step 6: Add the curry powder mix and stir until aromatic.
  7. Step 7: Add the chicken drumsticks and stir-fry for about 4 minutes.
  8. Step 8: Add hot water to cover the chicken and season with salt.
  9. Step 9: Cover the pan and simmer for about 20 minutes.
  10. Step 10: Turn off the heat and sprinkle garam masala before serving.
